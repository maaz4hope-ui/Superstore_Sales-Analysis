# 📊 Superstore Sales Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes Superstore sales data to identify key business insights and provide data-driven recommendations for revenue growth and profitability.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Clean and preprocess sales data
- Analyze sales, profit, and customer segments
- Identify top-performing categories and regions
- Provide actionable business recommendations

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- **Python** (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib)
- **Google Colab**

## 📈 Key Insights

### 💰 Overall Performance
- **Total Sales:** $2,297,200.86
- **Total Profit:** $286,397.02
- **Profit Margin:** 12.47%

### 📂 Category Performance
| Category | Sales | Profit | Status |
|----------|-------|--------|--------|
| Technology | $836,154 | $145,455 | ✅ Best |
| Office Supplies | $719,047 | $122,491 | ✅ Good |
| Furniture | $741,999 | $18,451 | ⚠️ Low Margin |

### 👥 Segment Performance
- **Consumer:** $1,161,401 (Highest)
- **Corporate:** $706,146
- **Home Office:** $429,653

### 📍 Region Performance
- **West:** $725,458 (Highest)
- **East:** $678,781
- **Central:** $501,240
- **South:** $391,722

### 📅 Trends
- **Monthly:** Sales peak in November-December (Holiday season)
- **Yearly:** Consistent growth year over year
- **Daily:** Weekend sales higher than weekdays

## ✅ Recommendations
1. Review furniture pricing strategy to improve profit margin
2. Run special promotions during November-December
3. Launch mid-week flash sales to boost weekday revenue
